Welcome to Just For You :-

Just For You is an Organization which helps the needy and the poor. We collect your donation amount and help the poor by providing them edcation, clothes,food and all the basic requirements.We started this organization on 2017 and we have succesfully helped more than 3000 people till now.

We hope to continue the same with your small donation.

A small amount can change the life of a child through education,nutrition,and basic protection.Come and join us to help the needy today!

"We make a living by what we get,we make a life by what we give."
